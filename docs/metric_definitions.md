# Metric Definitions

- Gross booking revenue: reservation value before discounts.
- Net booking revenue: gross booking revenue less discounts.
- Recognized revenue: net booking revenue recognized when a sailing is completed.
- Deferred revenue: cash collected for sailings that have not yet been recognized.
- Occupancy rate: sold passenger nights divided by available passenger nights.
- Booking pace: bookings grouped by lead-time bucket before departure.
- Cancellation rate: cancelled reservations divided by total reservations.
- AOP attainment: actual recognized revenue, occupancy, or bookings divided by the corresponding AOP target.
- Revenue per passenger night: recognized revenue divided by sold passenger nights.
- Campaign ROAS: attributed net booking revenue divided by campaign spend.
- Booking conversion proxy: bookings compared with targets or campaign spend when impression/session data is unavailable.

Payment date is not revenue recognition date. Refunds and cancellation penalties are modeled as cash events and surfaced separately for finance analysis.
